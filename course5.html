MBA (Master of Business Administration)

Eligibility: Graduation in any stream

Minimum Marks Required: 50% for General, 45% for Reserved Categories

Entrance Exam:CAT / MAT / CMAT / Institute-level Test

Duration: 2 Years

MBA focuses on leadership, business strategy, finance, HR, and entrepreneurship. Students can specialize in fields like Marketing, Finance, Operations, or International Business.

Scope:

Career in corporate management, consulting, startups, and public sector. Offers high growth in leadership and executive roles across industries.
